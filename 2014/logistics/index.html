Logistics

This page includes all the logistics details for Blueprint; read it carefully. If you have questions, try checking out the FAQ. If you still have a question, please email us at blueprint@hackmit.org.

Waivers

Before you check-in you'll need to sign a legal waiver and photo release. If you are under 18 years old, please have your parent or legal guardian fill these forms out.

You can either digitally sign the forms or print them out, sign them by hand, and scan them into a PDF.
Then submit your PDF below.

If we do not get your waiver by Feb 8, we will release your spot to other students.

Venue

Blueprint is being held at Google Cambridge, located at 5 Cambridge Center in Cambridge, MA. It is a block away from MIT's Stata Center and the Kendall/MIT T stop.

Drop-off and Pick-up

There will be a standing zone in front of the building for parents to drop off and pick up.

GPS Address:
Google Cambridge
5 Cambridge Center
Cambridge, MA

Drive yourself

There is street-side parking available in the area. Alternatively, there is a pay parking garage right behind 5 Cambridge Center.

Take the T

Google Cambridge is less than a block from the Kendall/MIT stop on the Red line.

What to bring

Laptop and charger, if you said you'd bring one.
You also must bring a valid student ID for admission (name must match registration).

Check-in and check-out procedures

You must have an official photo ID to check-in.

If a parent is picking you up, we suggest asking them to arrive at 10:30PM.
