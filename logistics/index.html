Logistics

This page includes all the logistics for our event; read it carefully. If you have questions, try checking out the FAQ. If you still have a question, please email us at blueprint@hackmit.org.

Schedule

12PMLunch

8PMHacking stops, judging starts; dinner
9:30PMClosing ceremony & awards
10:30PMEnd of event

Venue

The event is being held at Google Cambridge, a block away from MIT's Stata Center and main campus. Check-in will happen in the ground floor lobby, after which you will take the elevator to the fourth floor event space.

Drop-off and Pick-up

There will be a standing zone in front of the lobby.
GPS Address:
Google Cambridge
5 Cambridge Center
Cambridge, MA

Drive yourself

There is street-side parking available around the around. Alternatively, there is a parking garage right behind 5 Cambridge Center. It costs $23 for 3-24 hours.

Take the T

Google Cambridge is less than a block away from the Kendall/MIT stop on the Red line. Just look for the signs to Blueprint.

What to bring

Laptop and charger. All attendees are required to bring a valid student ID for admission (name must match registration).

Organized by TechX HackMIT, an MIT student organization.
